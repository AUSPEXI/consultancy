# Cold Outreach Templates

The whole point is that you run the Citation Probe first and lead with the prospect's real result. Fill every bracket with an actual number from the dashboard. If the gap isn't embarrassing, pick a different prospect.

**Before you send:** top up Claude and Grok API credits so the probe covers every engine. Work one niche at a time so the "[Competitor] got cited [Y]" line is real and consistent across your list.

---

## LinkedIn DM (send this first)

> Hi [Name], I ran a quick test this week: I asked ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity "[the buying question in their category]" [20] times each. [Their company] came up [X] times. [Competitor] came up [Y]. That gap is costing you deals you can't see in analytics, because AI recommendations leave no trace. I put the full breakdown (which engines, which competitors, why) into a short audit. Worth me sending it over?

---

## Email

>

## Follow-up (one only, 4 to 5 days later if no reply)

> Hi [Name], circling back once. The reason I flagged it: the [their company] vs [competitor] gap tends to widen, because once an engine settles on who to recommend it keeps recommending them. The audit shows how to interrupt that. Happy to send it over, or to walk you through your numbers on a quick call. Either way I'll leave it there.

---

## Working notes

- One niche at a time. Send the same category question to ten competitors in one vertical so your numbers are real and consistent, and you look like the specialist.
- Don't discount the audit on the call. The founding rate is the discount. If they hesitate on price, offer less scope, not a lower number.
- The audit is the wedge, the retainer is the business. End every walkthrough call with "I can hand you the fix list, or I can implement it for you monthly."
