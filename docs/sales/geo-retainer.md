# GEO Retainer

*The audit tells you where you stand once. The retainer is how you climb, and stay there.*

AI recommendations are not fixed. Every time you publish, a competitor publishes, or an engine refreshes its sources, the answer to "what's the best [your category]" can shift. Being cited this month does not mean being cited next month. Staying in the recommendation set takes continuous, measured work. That is what this is.

## What I do each month

1. **Probe.** Re-measure your Share of Voice across the engines and against your competitors, so we always work from current data, not last quarter's.
2. **Find the gaps.** Identify the specific buying queries where you are losing citations and to whom.
3. **Build the content.** Write cite-magnet content (precise, citable, numbered facts in the formats engines actually quote) targeted at those losing queries.
4. **Publish.** Get it live, structured correctly, and readable by the AI crawlers.
5. **Re-probe and report.** Measure the movement and send you a short, honest report: what went up, what didn't, and what we do next.
