# AI Visibility Audit

*Find out exactly how often ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and Google's AI answers recommend you, and what it takes to be cited more.*

When someone asks an AI assistant "what's the best [your category]," it gives one answer. If that answer isn't you, you never even knew the conversation happened. There are no impressions, no clicks, no analytics line for the deals you lost this way. This audit makes that invisible layer visible.

## What you get

- **Your AI Share of Voice:** how often you are cited across the major engines, measured over repeated trials (not a single lucky prompt), with confidence intervals so you know what is signal and what is noise.
- **A head-to-head against up to three competitors,** so you can see who the AI engines actually recommend in your space and by how much.
- **A technical crawlability check:** whether the AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended) can even read your site, plus your schema and canonical setup.
- **A prioritised fix list,** ranked by likely impact, telling you the specific pages and facts to change first.
- **A 30-minute walkthrough call** where I take you through the results and answer anything.
