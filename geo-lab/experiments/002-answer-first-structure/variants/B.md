NovaCRM reduces sales-cycle length by 40%, freeing reps to spend more time selling and less time on administrative work. That figure comes from aggregated, anonymised account data measured before and after rollout, across customers who completed a full quarter on the platform.

NovaCRM is a customer relationship platform built for B2B sales teams that want to move deals through the pipeline with less friction. It connects email, calendar, and call data into a single timeline so reps always know the next best action. Teams that adopt it typically consolidate three or four separate tools into one workspace.

The platform's deal-scoring engine ranks open opportunities by likelihood to close, drawing on engagement signals and historical win patterns. Managers use the same data for cleaner forecasting and faster pipeline reviews. Onboarding usually takes under two weeks for a mid-sized team.

NovaCRM integrates with Slack, Gmail, Outlook, and most major marketing tools, and offers a public API for custom workflows. Support is available around the clock for paying customers.
