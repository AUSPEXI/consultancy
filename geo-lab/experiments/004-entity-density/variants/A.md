# Variant A — Control

NovaCRM is a sales platform built to help teams close deals faster. It connects with the email tools and messaging apps most sales teams already use, so reps don't have to switch between windows during a busy day. Deals, contacts, and conversations all live in one place.

Our founder built the first version after years of selling software and watching good deals slip through the cracks. The goal was simple: surface the next best action for every rep, automatically.

NovaCRM follows recognised security and privacy standards, so enterprise buyers can adopt it with confidence. Data is encrypted, access is controlled, and the platform meets the compliance requirements that large organisations expect.

Teams that switched to NovaCRM report shorter sales cycles and cleaner pipeline data. Because it pulls activity from the tools reps live in, managers get an accurate picture of where every deal stands without chasing updates.

NovaCRM also integrates with popular analytics and support tools, so the whole company works from the same customer record.
