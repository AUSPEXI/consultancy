# Variant B — Treatment

NovaCRM is a sales platform built to help teams close deals faster. It connects with Gmail, Outlook, and Slack, so reps don't have to switch between windows during a busy day. Deals, contacts, and conversations sync directly from Salesforce into one place.

Founder Mara Whitfield built the first version after years of selling software and watching good deals slip through the cracks. The goal was simple: surface the next best action for every rep, automatically.

NovaCRM is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant, so enterprise buyers can adopt it with confidence. Data is encrypted, access is controlled, and the platform meets the compliance requirements that large organisations expect.

Teams that switched from HubSpot to NovaCRM report shorter sales cycles and cleaner pipeline data. Because it pulls activity from Slack and Outlook, managers get an accurate picture of where every deal stands without chasing updates.

NovaCRM also integrates with Tableau and Zendesk, so the whole company works from the same customer record.
