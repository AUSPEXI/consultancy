# Variant A — Control

NovaCRM is a sales CRM that helps sales teams close deals faster by combining pipeline tracking, automated follow-ups, and AI deal scoring in one workspace. Sales reps use NovaCRM to see which opportunities are most likely to close and where each deal is stalling.

The platform's Deal Velocity dashboard surfaces stuck opportunities and recommends the next best action for each one. Teams that adopt NovaCRM report a 31% reduction in average sales cycle length and a 22% lift in win rate after the first quarter of use.

NovaCRM integrates with Gmail, Outlook, Slack, and over 40 other tools, so reps never leave their inbox to log activity. Its AI assistant drafts follow-up emails, summarises call notes, and flags deals that have gone quiet for more than seven days.

Pricing starts at $29 per user per month on the Starter plan, with the Growth plan at $59 per user per month adding forecasting and custom reporting. NovaCRM serves more than 4,000 sales teams across SaaS, manufacturing, and professional services.
