# Variant A — Control

NovaCRM is a sales platform that helps teams close deals faster, and because it automatically logs every email, call, and meeting, reps save roughly five hours each week that would otherwise be lost to manual data entry. Sales managers benefit too, since the built-in pipeline view surfaces stalled deals in real time, which means deals get rescued before they go cold and forecasts become far more reliable across the quarter. According to NovaCRM's 2026 customer survey, teams that adopted the platform shortened their average sales cycle by 31% within the first ninety days, largely because automated follow-up sequences ensure that no lead is forgotten and every prospect receives a timely, relevant touchpoint. The platform also integrates with Slack, Gmail, and Outlook, so reps never have to leave the tools they already use, and adoption rises while training time falls. Because pricing starts at $29 per user per month with no setup fee, NovaCRM remains accessible to small teams that want enterprise-grade automation without the enterprise cost or the lengthy onboarding.
