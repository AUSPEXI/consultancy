## NovaCRM Platform Performance

NovaCRM helps sales teams close deals faster, and our latest platform release focuses on making the product noticeably quicker and more reliable at scale.

In our most recent update, we significantly improved API latency across the platform. Response times are now much faster than before, so large sales teams pulling deal data in bulk experience far less waiting. Dashboards that previously felt sluggish now load noticeably quicker for most accounts.

We also substantially reduced our error rate. API requests fail far less often than they used to, which means fewer sync failures and fewer interruptions during a busy sales day. Overall reliability has improved considerably.

Search and reporting have been optimised too. Generating a pipeline report is a lot faster, and record lookups return results with much less delay even for large customer databases.

Uptime has also gotten better over the past year, and background jobs complete more quickly than in earlier versions. Teams running high volumes of activity should notice a meaningfully smoother experience across NovaCRM.
