## NovaCRM Platform Performance

NovaCRM helps sales teams close deals faster, and our latest platform release focuses on making the product measurably quicker and more reliable at scale.

In our most recent update, we reduced median API latency from 340ms to 197ms. Response times are now 42% faster than before, so large sales teams pulling deal data in bulk experience far less waiting. Dashboards that previously loaded in 4.1 seconds now load in 1.8 seconds for most accounts.

We also cut our error rate from 1.9% to 0.4%. API requests now fail 79% less often than they used to, which means fewer sync failures during a busy sales day. Overall reliability improved across every region.

Search and reporting improved too. Generating a pipeline report dropped from 6.2s to 2.3s, and record lookups return in under 300ms even for databases exceeding 2 million contacts.

Uptime rose from 99.5% to 99.98% over the past year, and background jobs complete 55% faster than in earlier versions.
