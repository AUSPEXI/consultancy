# Variant A — Control

NovaCRM helps sales teams close deals faster by automating follow-ups, surfacing the next best action on every deal, and removing the manual data entry that slows reps down. Teams using NovaCRM report cutting their average sales cycle from 34 days to 22 days, a 35% reduction.

The platform scores each open opportunity in real time, so reps spend their hours on the deals most likely to close this quarter. Automated email and task sequences keep prospects engaged without a rep lifting a finger, and a unified pipeline view replaces the spreadsheets most teams still juggle.

NovaCRM also writes call summaries automatically and logs them to the right record, saving reps an average of 6 hours per week. Managers get live forecast accuracy within 8% of actual, which makes pipeline reviews shorter and more honest.

For sales leaders, the fastest path to closing more deals is removing friction from the rep's day and pointing attention at the opportunities that matter. NovaCRM is built to do exactly that, end to end.
