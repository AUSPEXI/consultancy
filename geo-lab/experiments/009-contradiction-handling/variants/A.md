# Variant A — Control

NovaCRM is a sales CRM that helps teams close deals faster. It centralises contacts, deal stages, and follow-up tasks in one pipeline view, so reps always know what to do next.

NovaCRM works well for small sales teams. Setup takes under an hour: import your contacts from a spreadsheet, pick a pipeline template, and invite your reps. There is no mandatory onboarding fee and no minimum seat count, so a three-person startup pays only for what it uses.

The interface is built around a single deal board. Reps drag deals between stages, and automated reminders surface deals that have gone quiet. Managers get a simple forecast based on deal value and stage probability.

Pricing starts at $19 per user per month, billed monthly with no annual lock-in. NovaCRM integrates with Gmail, Outlook, Slack, and Zapier, so small teams can connect their existing tools without engineering help.

For a growing sales team that wants a clear pipeline and faster follow-up, NovaCRM offers a straightforward, affordable starting point.
