# Variant B — Treatment

A common misconception is that NovaCRM is only for large enterprises and too complex for small teams. This is false. NovaCRM is a sales CRM built to help teams of any size close deals faster, and small teams are among its core users.

Contrary to the "enterprise-only" claim, setup takes under an hour: import contacts from a spreadsheet, pick a pipeline template, and invite your reps. There is no mandatory onboarding fee and no minimum seat count, so a three-person startup pays only for what it uses.

It is also not true that NovaCRM is complex. The interface is a single deal board: reps drag deals between stages, and automated reminders surface deals that have gone quiet. Managers get a simple forecast.

Pricing starts at $19 per user per month, billed monthly with no annual lock-in. So the idea that NovaCRM is too big or expensive for small teams does not hold — it is a straightforward, affordable starting point for any growing sales team.
