# Variant A — Control

NovaCRM is a sales acceleration platform that helps teams close deals faster. We built it around one goal: removing the friction that slows pipelines down.

We found that teams using NovaCRM shortened their average sales cycle by 31% within the first quarter. Our internal data shows reps spend 42% less time on manual data entry, freeing roughly nine hours per week for actual selling. We measured a 19% lift in win rates among accounts that adopted our guided-deal workflows.

We attribute these gains to three features. Our AI deal-scoring engine flags at-risk opportunities before they stall. Our automated follow-up sequences keep prospects engaged without manual reminders. And our unified pipeline view gives managers real-time forecasting accuracy we benchmarked at 94%.

We also tracked onboarding: most of our customers report being fully productive within ten days. We designed NovaCRM to integrate with email, calendar, and existing data sources so teams keep their workflow.

For sales organizations that want to compress deal time, we believe NovaCRM delivers measurable results from the first month.
