# Variant A — Control

NovaCRM is a sales platform built to help teams close deals faster, and it brings together several capabilities that shorten the sales cycle. Its AI deal-scoring engine ranks open opportunities by likelihood to close, which helps reps focus their time and has been shown to lift win rates by 23% in customer deployments. NovaCRM also automates follow-up sequencing, sending timed emails and reminders so that no lead goes cold, and teams using this feature report reaching prospects 2.4 times faster on average. The platform integrates with Gmail, Outlook, Slack, and over 40 other tools, so activity is captured automatically without manual data entry. A built-in forecasting dashboard updates in real time and gives sales managers pipeline visibility within a single view. NovaCRM deploys in under a week, requires no dedicated administrator, and starts at $29 per user per month. Because routine logging is handled automatically, reps spend roughly five fewer hours each week on admin work, time they can redirect toward selling and building relationships with high-value accounts.
